### My Open Source Manifesto ###
Date: 30 March 2026

I believe in open source because tools like LibreOffice - with
features like AUDIT - prove that world-class software can
be built and shared without charging anyone a single rupee.

To me, open source means choice. It is not just a licence;
it is a philosophy that says knowledge grows when it is shared.

If I had the chance to contribute, I would build Student random number generator
and release it freely, so that someone else can build on top of it.

- HP | 30 March 2026
